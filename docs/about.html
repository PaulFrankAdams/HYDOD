About HYDOD

HYDOD (Hydrogen-On-Demand) is an open-source initiative dedicated to creating accessible, efficient, and transparent hydrogen generation technology. It brings together electronics (like Arduino and Raspberry Pi), resonant electrolysis systems, and community-driven innovation.

HYDOD is committed to remaining free and unpatented so that no single person or organization can own or suppress the technology. It is licensed under the GNU GPL v3.0 to ensure that all derivative works remain open and publicly accessible.

This project is intended for scientists, makers, educators, and engineers who believe in open collaboration to solve global energy challenges. Learn more at hydod.com.
