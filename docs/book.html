No Carbon Required

No Carbon Required explores the science, suppressed history, and remarkable potential of hydrogen-based clean energy. From Nikola Tesla's theories to modern electrolysis breakthroughs, this book lays the foundation for the HYDOD open-source project.

Written for curious minds, researchers, and makers alike, it unpacks a vision of energy freedom driven by resonance and water—not fossil fuels.

About the Author

Paul Frank Adams is a technical author with a professional background in utilities, including oil, coal, and power distribution, as well as general engineering. With a deep curiosity for suppressed science and practical experimentation, Paul now focuses on clean energy innovation. Through the HYDOD project, he champions hydrogen-based systems that are accessible, collaborative, and transformative—built to serve the public rather than profit.
